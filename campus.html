Living Here

Our campus includes modern classrooms, advanced labs, sports fields, libraries, and student clubs that are active year-round.

Student life features:

  • Clubs and societies (debate, tech, arts, volunteerism)
  • Annual sports meets, cultural festivals, and innovation fairs
  • On-campus housing, dining, and health services

Support services include academic counseling, career placement, and mental wellness programs.

New this year: weekend community outreach events and a student startup incubator program.
