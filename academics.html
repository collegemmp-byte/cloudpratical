Welcome to MMP University

A dynamic university in Nepal offering strong academics, meaningful co-curriculars, and global connections.

Programs

MMP University offers engineering, computer science, business administration, and graduate research programs tailored to industry needs.

Departments include: Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Management, Applied Sciences, and Architecture. Course highlights: Data Science, Renewable Energy Systems, AI, Entrepreneurship, and Environmental Management.

Research: students and faculty collaborate on funded projects in robotics, sustainable development, and smart infrastructure.

New initiative: mentorship from alumni, interdisciplinary lab internships, and community project credits for all majors.
