Welcome to MMP University

A dynamic university in Nepal offering strong academics, meaningful co-curriculars, and global connections.

Our Story

MMP University started with a vision to deliver world-class education in Nepal and empower students to become global leaders.

Mission: provide high-quality academic programs and practical skills while fostering innovation and ethical leadership.

Vision: be a top-tier center for research and community transformation through active industry partnerships.

We are expanding international exchange programs with partner universities in Asia and Europe for 2026-2027.
